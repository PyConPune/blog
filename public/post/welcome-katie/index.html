Welcome Katie Cunningham

November 23, 2016

Welcome Katie Cunningham as our keynote speaker for PyCon Pune 2017.

Katie Cunningham is a Python developer who is also a regular speaker, teacher, and writer of technical books. When she’s not cursing at computers, Keynote, or poorly formatted chapters, she knits, sews, and secretly writes fantasy books.

She is the CTO of Speak Agent, a company focused on helping kids learn English through games. There, she creates iPad apps, websites, and curses English grammar on a regular basis.

Katie lives in the DC area with her husband, two kids, and two dogs. You can follow her on Twitter at @kcunning
