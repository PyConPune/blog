A new Mailing List for us

June 08, 2017

PyCon Pune 2017 was a grand success. Now, its time to set the tune for PyCon Pune 2018.

Since our first endeavor it has always been a community driven event and we wish to be so. To promote the goal, Sayan Chowdhury has announced the call for the design volunteers, helping in building our 2018 website. To give a shape to our 2018 edition we, the community at large, need to communicate. Now we have a new space for discussion, the PyCon Pune mailing list. All the conversation, exchange of ideas related to our 2018 and also for all our future editions will be happening here. Everyone is welcome to join.

Together lets make the dream of a triumphant PyCon Pune 2018 a shining truth.
